LASAGNA RECIPE

Description:

This produced some really delicious lasagna. I doubled the sauce because I could tell it wasnâ€™t going to be enough.
I also used ricotta cheese in place of the cottage cheese. Will make again

Ingredients:

  • 2 beaten Eggs
  • 1 cup of sieved Wheat Flour
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoonful white sugar
  • 1 cup schemed milk
  • Ground cheese

Steps:

  1. Beat two Eggs in a container and whisk to mix well
  2. Add a pinch and salt and 1 teaspoonful of sugar
  3. Stir the mixture to mix evenly
  4. Add 1 cup of milk into the mixture
  5. Add wheat flour while stiring and mixing until forms a smooth dough
  6. Leave to rest for 10 minutes
  7. Spread the mixture evenly on a microwave pan and heat under low heat for 30 minutes
  8. Turn off the heat and remove from oven. Spread cheese sprinkles
